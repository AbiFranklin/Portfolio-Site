Welcome friend. A bit about myself...

I was born and raised in southeast Michigan. The daughter of an artist and a computer programmer. My journey with computers began before I could talk. In 1st grade, my dad taught me Fox Pro. I took notes in my Lisa Frank notebnook. I went on to study the completely unrelated field of Biology at the University of Michigan. Post graduation, I fell into environmental compliance and safety. My husband's career eventually brought us to coastal Texas.

My wanderlust and desire for a more portable career lead me back to my original love for computers - coding & deisgn.

And that is where I am now.
